About Us

At Kuliner, we celebrate Indonesia's rich culinary heritage through refined flavors, warm hospitality, and an ambiance inspired by the archipelago's timeless beauty. Each dish is a tribute to tradition — crafted with care, elevated with elegance, and served with pride.

More than a dining destination, Kuliner is a journey — where every bite tells a story of culture, spice, and soul.

Our Signature Dishes

Where tradition meets artistry — taste the essence of Indonesia on every plate

Rendang

Slow-braised beef in coconut milk, infused with a complex blend of traditional spices. This iconic West Sumatran dish boasts a deep, rich flavor and melt-in-your-mouth texture.

Rawon

A soulful East Javanese black beef soup, enriched with the earthy depth of keluak (black nut), lemongrass, and galangal. Warm, aromatic, and deeply comforting.

Ayam Taliwang

Spicy grilled chicken from Lombok, marinated in chilies, garlic, and shrimp paste. Expertly grilled for a smoky finish—bold, aromatic, and irresistibly flavorful.
