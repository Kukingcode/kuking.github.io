Our Story

Founded in 2023

"Kuliner" was born from a passion for authentic Indonesian flavors and a dream to bring the warmth of Indonesian hospitality to the world.

The name "Kuliner," which translates to "Cuisine," embodies our commitment to crafting traditional dishes with a modern touch, using only the freshest ingredients.

Our goal is to create not just a dining experience, but a journey through the rich tapestry of Indonesian culture, one plate at a time.

Cultural Heritage, Timeless Values

"At Kuliner, we don’t just serve food, we share a legacy. Every dish, every experience, is rooted in the values of Indonesian culture — respect, hospitality, and a deep connection to the land."

Innovation with Integrity

"We believe in innovation, but not at the cost of tradition. Kuliner combines the best of Indonesia’s culinary history with contemporary techniques and flavors."

Sustainability at Our Core

"Sustainability is not just a trend, it’s a principle. At Kuliner, we prioritize eco-friendly practices, from sourcing ingredients locally to using sustainable materials."

Passion and Dedication in Every Dish

"At Kuliner, our team is the heart of every experience. From the chefs in the kitchen to the servers at your table, each person brings passion, dedication, and a love for Indonesian culture into every dish."

Our Signature Dishes

Where tradition meets artistry — taste the essence of Indonesia on every plate

Rendang

Slow-braised beef in coconut milk, infused with a complex blend of traditional spices. This iconic West Sumatran dish boasts a deep, rich flavor and melt-in-your-mouth texture, offering an unforgettable taste of Indonesia's culinary heritage.

Rawon

A soulful East Javanese black beef soup, enriched with the earthy depth of keluak (black nut), lemongrass, and galangal. Served warm and aromatic, Rawon delivers a comforting yet bold flavor experience — a timeless taste of tradition in every spoonful.

Ayam Taliwang

Spicy grilled chicken hailing from the island of Lombok, marinated in a fiery blend of chilies, garlic, and shrimp paste. Expertly grilled for a smoky finish, Ayam Taliwang is bold, aromatic, and irresistibly flavorful — perfect for lovers of heat and heart.
